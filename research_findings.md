## SOCIAL MEDIA & MARKETING ENVIRONMENT ANALYSIS

### Instagram Algorithm & Strategy (2025)

#### Algorithm Mechanics
- **Reels dominate**: Average engagement rate 1.23% for Reels vs 0.70% for image posts
- **Ideal Reel length**: 30-90 seconds (long enough for substance, short enough for retention)
- **Engagement quality hierarchy**: Saves + Shares > Likes + Comments (most valuable)
- **Speed matters**: First 30-60 minutes determine overall performance
- **Content type ranking**: Reels > Everything else (feed posts, stories, etc.)

#### Content Pillars for Wellness Brands
Successful health coaches typically focus on 2-3 content pillars:
1. **Educational content** (tips, science, how-tos)
2. **Inspirational/motivational content** (transformation stories, mindset)
3. **Personal/relatable content** (day-in-life, behind-the-scenes, vulnerability)
4. **Engagement content** (questions, polls, community building)

#### Growth Strategies for Health Coaches
- **Mix content formats**: Reels, Stories, Carousels, static posts
- **Consistency over perfection**: Regular posting beats perfect but sporadic
- **Value-first approach**: Provide actionable tips, recipes, insights
- **Community engagement**: Respond to comments, engage with followers' content
- **Clear positioning**: Define unique approach, personal experience, target audience
- **Visual storytelling**: Use before/after, process videos, lifestyle imagery

### Email Marketing & Funnel Strategy

#### Essential Email Sequences
1. **Welcome sequence**: Introduce yourself, provide quick win, build trust
2. **Educational sequence**: Deliver value, establish authority
3. **Nurture sequence**: Build relationship, share stories, address objections
4. **Sales sequence**: Present offer, handle objections, create urgency
5. **Post-purchase sequence**: Onboarding, support, upsell opportunities
6. **Re-engagement sequence**: Win back inactive subscribers

#### Lead Magnet Ideas for Health/Nutrition Coaches
- Free grocery shopping lists (tailored to specific diets)
- Meal planning templates
- Hormone balance checklists
- 7-day meal plans
- Video tutorials on nutrition basics
- PDF guides on specific health topics
- Quiz to identify health issues
- Free mini-course or webinar

#### Email Marketing Best Practices
- **Segmentation**: Tailor messages to different audience segments
- **Personalization**: Use subscriber names, reference their interests
- **Clear subject lines**: Increase open rates
- **Mobile optimization**: Most emails read on phones
- **Call-to-action**: Clear, compelling next steps
- **Automation**: Set up sequences that run without manual intervention

### Paid Advertising Strategy (Meta/Facebook/Instagram)

#### 2025 Meta Advertising Best Practices
- **Full-funnel approach**: Awareness → Consideration → Conversion
- **Creative testing**: Test multiple ad creatives, copy angles, audiences
- **Give Meta options**: Provide multiple versions for algorithm to optimize
- **Don't get emotionally attached**: Let data guide decisions
- **Targeting**: Let Meta's algorithm do the targeting with good creative
- **Mobile-first**: Optimize all ads for mobile viewing

#### Digital Product Ad Strategy
- **Typical ROI**: 60% from paid ads, 40% from organic (Maria Wendt model)
- **Low-ticket products**: $7-$27 price points work well with ads
- **Retargeting**: Target website visitors who didn't convert
- **Lookalike audiences**: Target people similar to existing customers
- **Testing budget**: Allocate 20-30% of ad budget to testing new angles

#### Health Coaching Specific Ad Strategies
- **Lead generation ads**: Capture emails for funnel
- **Free consultation offers**: Build sales pipeline
- **Webinar promotions**: Educational content with sales component
- **Transformation stories**: Before/after testimonials
- **Problem-agitation-solution**: Address pain points directly
