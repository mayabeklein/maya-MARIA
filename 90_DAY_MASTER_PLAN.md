**Challenge 1: Low conversion rates**

- **Backup:** Offer payment plans, lower prices temporarily

- **Solution:** Clean list regularly, use double opt-in, monitor spam complaints
- **Backup:** Diversify to SMS marketing

- **Solution:** Follow all platform policies, avoid aggressive claims
- **Backup:** Focus on organic growth, influencer partnerships

- **Solution:** Set clear expectations, provide excellent support
- **Backup:** Offer partial refunds, credit toward other products

**Challenge 5: Content creation burnout**
- **Solution:** Batch content, repurpose existing, hire help
- **Backup:** Reduce posting frequency, focus on quality over quantity

**Challenge 6: Technical issues**
- **Solution:** Test everything before launch, have backup systems
- **Backup:** Manual delivery if automation fails
